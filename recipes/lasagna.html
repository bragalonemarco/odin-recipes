Lasagna

This is an easy recipe for a classic lasagna.

Just a few steps and your sunday lunch will have 'nonna's taste!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
